About Me

Hi, my name is Lanbo Shi, but my friends call me Sander. I was born in Holland, grew up in Northern Ireland, completed my high school studies in Hangzhou, China, and am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. My interests range from music, sports and modern languages to website developing, and I have always been fascinated by software development and creating software that can be beneficial to our world.
