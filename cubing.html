Speed Cubing Videos

Solving a Rubik's cube is fun, but speed-cubing is even more so. It gives you the challenge of coming up with different techniques to become faster and beat your own personal records!

2 x 2 x 2 (The Pocket Cube)

This is my 2x2x2 Rubik's Cube Solve. My time is 9.19 seconds, around 6 times that of the world record (as of July, 2016): 1.51 seconds (2015, Lucas Etter).

3 x 3 x 3 (The Rubik's Cube)

This is my 3x3x3 Rubik's Cube Solve, using the Fridrich Method. My time is 17.82 seconds, around 3 times that of the world record (as of July, 2016): 6.54 seconds (2013, Feliks Zemdegs).

4 x 4 x 4 (Rubik's Revenge)

This is my 4x4x4 Rubik's Cube Solve. My time is 2 minutes and 27.54 seconds, around 6 times that of the world record (as of July, 2016): 26.03 seconds (2014, Sebastian Weyer).

5 x 5 x 5 (The Professor's Cube)

This is my 5x5x5 Rubik's Cube Solve. My time is 4 minutes and 24.90 seconds, a little over 5 times that of the world record (as of July, 2016): 50.15 seconds (2015, Feliks Zemdegs).

6 x 6 x 6

This is my 6x6x6 Rubik's Cube Solve. My time is 8 minutes and 53.72 seconds, around 5 times that of the world record (as of July, 2016): 1 minute and 43.83 seconds (2015, Feliks Zemdegs).
