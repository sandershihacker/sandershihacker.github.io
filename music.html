Piano Videos

I greatly enjoy music and love to spend time learning to play pieces I like on the piano and guitar.

Yu Zhou Chang Wan (渔舟唱晚)

Yu Zhou Chang Wan (Fisherman's Serenade) is a famous Chinese piece, originally played on the Guzheng. This is a simple piano version.

The Meadow

The Meadow, a lovely piece which I find to be very soothing to play and to listen to, is composed by Alexandre Desplat. I am still working on slowing down my pace, so it does not seem as if I am in a hurry to go somewhere right after I finish playing the last note.

Marriage D'amour

Marriage D'amour is one of my favourite pieces by Richard Clayderman. Clayderman himself carries it out at quite a merry pace, whereas many others prefer to use a slower pace. If my version seems as if I am racing through it, I did not intend for it to be like that.
